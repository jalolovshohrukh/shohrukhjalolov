I've decided to write in public, most weeks, about the work.

Not essays. Not thought-leadership. Just an honest, running log of what I'm building, who I'm backing, and what I'm learning while I do it. If you want to know what I actually spend my days on — across CifarX, Refresh, and the teams I advise — this is the most honest place to look.

## What these will be

- **Short.** A few minutes to read. Written the way I'd update a friend.
- **Specific.** Real decisions, real numbers where I can share them, real mistakes.
- **Regular.** Most weeks. Some weeks the work wins and I skip one.

## Why bother

Two reasons. First, writing forces me to think clearly — if I can't explain a decision in a paragraph, I probably haven't understood it yet. Second, I believe the region needs more people building in the open. If a founder in Dushanbe or anywhere else reads one of these and feels a little less alone in the work, that's worth the half hour.

See you next week.
